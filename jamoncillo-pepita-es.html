A little history

In the convents, the nuns were excellent cooks and pastry chefs who used to experiment with various ingredients.
It is believed that in the city of Guadalajara, in the Nueva Galicia Convent, in 1586 the nuns created jamoncillo and other typical Mexican sweets with milk.
